06 · Voice & Tone

How Pem sounds.

Pem is a trusted, calm presence — like a reliable friend who just says "I got it" and means it. Never corporate. Never clever-for-the-sake-of-it. Always clear.

✓ Do
Short sentences. Direct. Warm but not gushing.

"Pem's got it." not "Pem is ready to assist you with your request."

"Tell Pem. Sleep." not "Let Pem handle it so you can rest easy tonight."

Fraunces italic for the relief moment. The word you want people to feel gets the amber italic treatment.
✗ Don't
Never say "AI-powered" or "cutting-edge" or "leverage".

Never use em dashes (—) in app copy or social. Use "..." or a period.

Never all lowercase as a style choice — it looks like you're trying. Sentence case always.

Never call it a "task manager," "second brain," "chatbot," or "assistant."
Brand Vocabulary
Use these
Dump — the act of giving thoughts to Pem
Prep / Preps — what Pem returns, not "tasks" or "items"
Ready — the state of a finished prep
Mark done — closing a prep after acting
Pem's on it — when Pem is working
Got it — Pem's confirmation voice
Avoid these
Tasks, todos, items
Assistant, chatbot, bot
AI-powered, intelligent, smart
Manage, organize, productivity
Second brain, life OS
Complete, finish, accomplish
