How Pem organizes your thoughts
🎯
Extracts what matters
Pem reads your messy dump and pulls out every actionable item. You don't structure anything — Pem does.
"...also the gym thing, and mom's birthday, and I need to call John..."
📬
Builds your inbox
Items land in a smart inbox — organized by urgency, deadline, and what's overdue. Like email, but for your thoughts.
Today · This week · Someday. Always clear, never overwhelming.
🛒
Groups similar things
Three shopping items across three different dumps? Pem creates one shopping list automatically. You tackle them together.
Coffee, olive oil, toothpaste → Shopping list · 3 items
✏️
Drafts when it can
When Pem has enough context — the email you've been putting off, the cancellation message — it drafts it. You copy and send.
"Write to my landlord about the leak." → Draft ready.
